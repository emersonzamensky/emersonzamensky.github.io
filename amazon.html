Amazon Waste
We were given a list of topics to choose from for the focus of this project. This list was comprised of massive, ongoing problems in environmental science and policy most of which directly related to a certain company. Our group chose to research Amazon's waste problem, which was hefty. There were three parts to this project; the full report, the back of the envelope calculation (BOE for short), and the presentation (below you will find all three). The BOE made this project rather interesting because it allowed me to better grasp the weight of the problem and the impact our solution would have.
