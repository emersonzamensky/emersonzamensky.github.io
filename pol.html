Resource and Food Preservation: Utilization of Hydroponic Systems in Place of Conventional Agriculture
This project required us to create an original policy brief tackling a major environmental issue. I chose to replace traditional agriculture practices with a special type of hydroculture that grows plants using a nutrient/water solution in place of soil. Finding alternative green replacements to otherwise environmentally damaging practices is a huge interest of mine within environmental science and policy. Although the formatting of this project was a little tricky on Word, I enjoyed completing this and am happy with the end result.
